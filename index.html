    Who Qualifies

    Eligibility Requirements

    Most U.S. citizens and permanent residents qualify for at least one program.

    • ✔ U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident
    • ✔ Annual household income below $95,000
    • ✔ Currently residing in the United States
    • ✔ Valid Social Security Number (SSN)
    • ✔ Demonstrated financial hardship or need
    • ✔ No outstanding federal fraud convictions
    • ✔ Minimum age of 18 years
    • ✔ Up-to-date federal tax filing status
